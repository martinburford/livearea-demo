# Grid

The `<Grid />` component works in a slightly different way to all other components, in as much it as has 2 exports, `<Col>` and `<Row>`. Whilst a `<Row>` acts merely as a containing element, each `<Col>` can receive its own props (as below):

### **Component props**

| Name           | Description                                                 | Default | Values                                                                                                               |
|----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|:-------:|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| mobile.span    | How many columns to span within the mobile breakpoint       | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| mobile.offset  | How many columns to offset by within the mobile breakpoint  | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| span           | How many columns to span a column (on a fixed % basis)      | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| offset         | How many columns to offset by (on a fixed % basis)          | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| tablet.span    | How many columns to span within the tablet breakpoint       | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| tablet.offset  | How many columns to offset by within the tablet breakpoint  | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| desktop.span   | How many columns to span within the desktop breakpoint      | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
| desktop.offset | How many columns to offset by within the desktop breakpoint | -       | <code>1 &#124; 2 &#124; 3 &#124; 4 &#124; 5 &#124; 6 &#124; 7 &#124; 8 &#124; 9 &#124; 10 &#124; 11 &#124; 12</code> |
